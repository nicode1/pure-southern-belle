    About Pure Southern Belle LLC

    Pure Southern Belle LLC was launched out of very humble beginnings. It was originally founded in 2016 under the name Southern Belle Photo Boothe and focused on creating photo booth backdrops for baby showers, church events, birthday parties, and other events. The business spawned from a request by a family member to utilize their expertise, but that soon turned into increased demand from a growing clientele seeking Pure Southern Belle LLC’s precise craftsmanship. In late 2018, a shift came. With a name change, a new logo, and new products being marketed and sold, the business took a turn in a new direction. Southern Belle Photo Boothe then became what it is today...

    Lynnea Moniqué...is a visionary...a lover of uniqueness...a crafter for the people! Having the love of Southern culture since a child, Lynnea Moniqué’s sense of the culture combined with pizzazz and sass can be seen in every detail in her work.
